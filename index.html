Enzo

Enzo 3D

Enzo is a node-based procedural 3D modeling framework for Visual Effects and Animation.

It was created as an accessible alternative to existing industry tools, which are often prohibitively expensive, closed source, and resistant to user driven innovation. Enzo is currently designed as a proof of concept and hobby project. The goal is to build a lean functional foundation for further development, experimentation, and community contribution.
